Featured Report

Incorporating Environmental, Social and Governance Factors Into Fixed Income Investment is the result of a partnership between the World Bank Group and Government Pension Investment Fund (GPIF) of Japan: to collaborate on initiatives that promote strategies for including environmental, social and governance (ESG) criteria in investment decisions across asset classes.

Contributing Programs

The ESG Data Project is the result of collaboration between the Global Program on Sustainability (GPS) and the Development Data Group with support from the Wealth Accounting and Valuation of Ecosystem Services (WAVES) Program and the Human Capital Project.

Contributing team members are contributors to the Coalition of Finance Ministers for Climate Action (CAPE), the Network for Greening the Financial System (NGFS), and the Sustainable Banking Network (SBN). The work of these networks feeds into the work of the ESG data team.
