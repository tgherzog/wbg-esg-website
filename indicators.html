--- title: List of ESG Indicators ---

The table below shows the indicators available in the draft ESG dataset:

Environment Pillar
  • Emissions & pollution
    • Methane emissions (metric tons of CO2 equivalent per capita)
    • Nitrous oxide emissions (metric tons of CO2 equivalent per capita)
    • PM2.5 air pollution, mean annual exposure (micrograms per cubic meter)
    • CO2 emissions (metric tons per capita)
    • GHG net emissions/removals by LUCF (Mt of CO2 equivalent)
  • Natural capital endowment & management
    • Terrestrial and marine protected areas (% of total territorial area)
    • Mammal species, threatened
    • Forest area (% of land area)
    • Annual freshwater withdrawals, total (% of internal resources)
    • Adjusted savings: net forest depletion (% of GNI)
    • Adjusted savings: natural resources depletion (% of GNI)
  • Energy use & security
    • Energy imports, net (% of energy use)
    • Electricity production from coal sources (% of total)
    • Renewable electricity output (% of total electricity output)
    • Energy intensity level of primary energy (MJ/$2011 PPP GDP)
    • Energy use (kg of oil equivalent per capita)
    • Renewable energy consumption (% of total final energy consumption)
    • Fossil fuel energy consumption (% of total)
  • Environment/climate risk & resilience
    • Droughts, floods, extreme temperatures (% of population, average 1990-2009)
    • Population density (people per sq. km of land area)
  • Food security
    • Agricultural land (% of land area)
    • Food production index (2004-2006 = 100)
    • Agriculture, forestry, and fishing, value added (% of GDP)
Social Pillar
  • Education & Skills
    • Literacy rate, adult total (% of people ages 15 and above)
    • School enrollment, primary (% gross)
    • Government expenditure on education, total (% of government expenditure)
  • Employment
    • Unemployment, total (% of total labor force) (modeled ILO estimate)
    • Labor force participation rate, total (% of total population ages 15-64) (modeled ILO estimate)
    • Children in employment, total (% of children ages 7-14)
  • Demography
    • Population ages 65 and above (% of total)
    • Life expectancy at birth, total (years)
    • Fertility rate, total (births per woman)
  • Poverty & inequality
    • Poverty headcount ratio at national poverty lines (% of population)
    • Income share held by lowest 20%
    • GINI index (World Bank estimate)
    • Annualized average growth rate in per capita real survey mean consumption or income, total population (%)
  • Health & nutrition
    • Prevalence of undernourishment (% of population)
    • Mortality rate, under-5 (per 1,000 live births)
    • Cause of death, by communicable diseases and maternal, prenatal and nutrition conditions (% of total)
    • Prevalence of overweight (% of adults)
    • Hospital beds (per 1,000 people)
  • Access to services
    • Access to electricity (% of population)
    • People using safely managed drinking water services (% of population)
    • People using safely managed sanitation services (% of population)
    • Access to clean fuels and technologies for cooking (% of population)
Governance Pillar
  • Human rights
    • Strength of legal rights index (0=weak to 12=strong)
    • Voice and Accountability: Estimate
  • Government Effectiveness
    • Government Effectiveness: Estimate
    • Regulatory Quality: Estimate
  • Stability & Rule of Law
    • Net migration
    • Political Stability and Absence of Violence/Terrorism: Estimate
    • Control of Corruption: Estimate
    • Rule of Law: Estimate
  • Economic Environment
    • Individuals using the Internet (% of population)
    • GDP growth (annual %)
    • Ease of doing business index (1=most business-friendly regulations)
  • Gender
    • Proportion of seats held by women in national parliaments (%)
    • Ratio of female to male labor force participation rate (%) (modeled ILO estimate)
    • School enrollment, primary and secondary (gross), gender parity index (GPI)
    • Unmet need for contraception (% of married women ages 15-49)
  • Innovation
    • Scientific and technical journal articles
    • Patent applications, residents
    • Research and development expenditure (% of GDP)
